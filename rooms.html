Accomodations

Experience ultimate privacy and relaxation in our thatched guest cabins. Each cabin is outfitted with a fully equipped bathroom, a private lounge area equipped with a comfortable hammock, and is within close proximity to the Rupununi River so you could take a dip at your discretion. Our rooms are available with different bed configurations to ensure comfort with even the largest of parties.
