Wilderness Adventure


Track through the environment, discover, and photograph plants and landmark scenes.


Book Now

Experience the Rupununi to its fullest! Go horseback riding, offroad driving, trail blazing, tracking, canoeing, camping, and more one this customizable package.

Day One

Get picked up at Lethem and be driven across the savannahs in a rustic 4x4. Settle into your rooms and maybe take a dip or stroll along the Rupununi river. Grab a torchlight and witness the nightlife around the Rupununi River. See noctural birds, fish and anything else we may encounter. Listen to the stories of decades of Rupununi adventures whilst having a delicious dinner with your hosts in the main dining hall.

Day Two

Enjoy a delcious breakfast made with local ingredients in the main dining hall. Be adventurous and blaze your own trails through the bush using a cutless. Follow the tracks of the local wildlife or look for the bubbles of Rupununi aquatic creatures. Return to the Lodge to relax and have lunch. Maybe go for a stroll or have a nap in hammock! Canoe yourself or boat down the river, and experience the beautful Rupununi sunset as we make camp, eat, and share stories around an open campfire.

Day Three

Get some offroad experience and take the wheel of one of our classic offroaders. Later we will go horseback riding, and if you're brave enough maybe attempt to ride a bull. Afterwards relax for a while, pack, and we'll return you to Lethem for further transport

This itinerary acts as an example. Your hobbies, budget, timeframe, and weather and circumstances will all influence our activities. Pricing for more than 4 persons available upon request.
